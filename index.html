Pic

Pic is the gallery site to display some beautiful picnic photos in TOKYO. Our city is highly developed and urbanized but too difficult to discover any open spaces which may feel free to have a time. This site provides good open spaces and times through these photos.
Ruriko Iizuka is the photographer in KEIO Pincic Club and takes the picnic photos and presents them in this site.
